About Me

I am a music producer and audio engineer who specializes in sound design, songwriting, audio mixing and editing audio. I graduated with honours from Harris Institute for the Arts in Toronto, studying Audio Production and Engineering. I’m currently working with industry professionals and dedicated artists at Mardox Studio in Toronto, Canada. As a solo artist my passion lies within the realm of electronic and hip-hop music. I’m continuously expanding my sound by fusing various genres and actively experimenting with synthesizers and sampling techniques to achieve unique, quality mixes.
